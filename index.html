at Kochs School is like!

8:30-10:30
Science & Math

School begins every day at 8:30. Today we follow Ki1. The class’ two first subjects are Science and Math. Today's topic in math is basic algebra.

10:30-11:30
Lunchbreak

First two hours of the school day is over. Time for lunch and the first break of the day! At the breaks the international and danish students gets to play with each other.

11:30-12:30
Math & English

Next up is Math and English. Today’s English class is about fairy tales. Usually the subjects are taught in both English and Danish to teach kids both languages.

12:30-13:00 Afternoon break

A quick break. The children get a chance to clear their heads and get ready for another hour of English class.

13:00-14:00
English class

Today’s last class. The class rounds up todays subject about fairy tales and H.C. Andersen's “The little match girl”.

That was it for today!
